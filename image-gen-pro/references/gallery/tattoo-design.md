Source: adapted from `gpt_image_2_skill` gallery category `Tattoo Design`; prompts are rewritten for local use.

## Linework Tattoo

- Example canvas: `1024x1024`
- Quality: `medium`

```text
Create a tattoo linework design of [subject].
Style: clean black line art, balanced negative space, stencil-ready silhouette.
Composition: centered design, no skin background unless requested.
Details: controlled ornament and readable shape.
Avoid: tiny fragile details, fake text, hateful symbols.
```

## Blackwork / Geometric

- Example canvas: `1024x1024`
- Quality: `medium`

```text
Create a blackwork geometric tattoo design for [motif].
Structure: symmetry or deliberate asymmetry, bold filled areas, crisp edges.
Texture: solid black with optional dotwork shading.
Avoid: gradients that cannot tattoo well, random lettering, overcomplication.
```
