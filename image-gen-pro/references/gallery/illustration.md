# Illustration Gallery

> Load when: 需要泛插画、书籍插图、编辑插图、概念插画或非摄影视觉。
> Avoid: 精确 UI、数据图、真实摄影或局部编辑。
> Pairs with: `../methods/style.md`、`../methods/composition.md`。

Source: adapted from `gpt_image_2_skill` gallery category `Illustration`; prompts are rewritten for local use.

## Editorial Illustration

- Example canvas: `1536x1024`
- Quality: `medium` or `high`

```text
Create an editorial illustration about [topic].
Concept: one clear visual metaphor, not a collage of ideas.
Composition: strong focal point, balanced negative space.
Style: [flat/textured/hand-drawn/vector], limited palette.
Avoid: generic stock icons, fake text, overloaded symbolism.
```

## Storybook Scene

- Example canvas: `1536x1024`
- Quality: `high`

```text
Create a storybook illustration of [scene].
Mood: warm narrative moment, expressive characters or objects.
Composition: readable scene depth, inviting details.
Style: textured illustration with cohesive palette.
Avoid: scary realism, cluttered tiny details, captions.
```
