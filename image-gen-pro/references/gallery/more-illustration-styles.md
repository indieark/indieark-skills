# More Illustration Styles Gallery

> Load when: 需要快速选择插画媒介，如剪纸、黏土、矢量、版画、儿童插画等。
> Avoid: 用户已经指定具体媒介，或任务是 API/CLI 维护。
> Pairs with: `../methods/style.md`、`../methods/composition.md`。

Source: adapted from `gpt_image_2_skill` gallery category `More Illustration Styles`; prompts are rewritten for local use.

## Material Illustration

- Size: `1024x1024`
- Quality: `medium`

```text
Create an illustration of [subject] in [paper cut/clay/felt/woodblock/vector] style.
Material rules: [texture, edges, shadows] match the chosen medium.
Composition: simple focal subject, clean background.
Palette: limited and cohesive.
Avoid: mixing unrelated media, fake text, photographic realism.
```

## Children's Illustration

- Size: `1536x1024`
- Quality: `medium`

```text
Create a children's book illustration of [scene].
Mood: friendly, warm, clear emotion.
Style: soft shapes, readable characters, gentle texture.
Composition: simple story moment with space to breathe.
Avoid: scary realism, clutter, text captions.
```
