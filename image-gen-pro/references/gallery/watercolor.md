# Watercolor Gallery

> Load when: 需要水彩、柔和纸面纹理、旅行手账、植物/建筑/食物插画。
> Avoid: 精确文字、UI、硬表面产品渲染或高对比科幻图。
> Pairs with: `../methods/style.md`、`../methods/composition.md`。

Source: adapted from `gpt_image_2_skill` gallery category `Watercolor`; prompts are rewritten for local use.

## Botanical Watercolor

- Example canvas: `1024x1536`
- Quality: `high`

```text
Create a watercolor illustration of [plant/flower/natural subject].
Paper: visible cold-press texture, soft pigment edges.
Composition: centered specimen or gentle arrangement with breathing room.
Color: transparent washes, subtle granulation.
Avoid: hard digital gradients, fake labels, crowded background.
```

## Travel Sketch

- Example canvas: `1536x1024`
- Quality: `medium`

```text
Create a watercolor travel sketch of [place/scene].
Composition: loose perspective, charming imperfections, light ink accents.
Palette: muted local colors, soft sky or paper background.
Mood: calm observation.
Avoid: photorealism, heavy black outlines, text annotations.
```
