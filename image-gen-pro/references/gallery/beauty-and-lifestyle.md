# Beauty & Lifestyle Gallery

> Load when: 需要美妆、护肤、生活方式、健康感、日常场景或社媒质感。
> Avoid: 医疗声明、真实品牌仿制、敏感人体改变承诺。
> Pairs with: `../methods/photography.md`、`../methods/product.md`、`../methods/style.md`。

Source: adapted from `gpt_image_2_skill` gallery category `Beauty & Lifestyle`; prompts are rewritten for local use.

## Beauty Product Lifestyle

- Example canvas: `1024x1536`
- Quality: `high`

```text
Create a beauty lifestyle image for [product/routine].
Scene: clean bathroom/vanity/studio setup, soft tactile props.
Lighting: soft natural or studio glow, flattering highlights.
Product: clearly visible hero, readable shape, no fake label text.
Avoid: medical claims, unrealistic skin, copied brand packaging.
```

## Wellness Moment

- Example canvas: `1536x1024`
- Quality: `medium`

```text
Create a lifestyle photograph of [wellness moment].
Mood: calm, believable, not overly staged.
Composition: human action or object ritual with clean environment.
Palette: gentle natural colors.
Avoid: exaggerated transformation claims, fake text, cluttered props.
```
