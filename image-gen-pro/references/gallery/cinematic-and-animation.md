# Cinematic & Animation Gallery

> Load when: 需要电影感、动画短片画面、场景 establishing shot 或故事瞬间。
> Avoid: 只要商品主图、UI mockup 或精确技术图。
> Pairs with: `../methods/composition.md`、`../methods/style.md`、`../methods/photography.md`。

Source: adapted from `gpt_image_2_skill` gallery category `Cinematic & Animation`; prompts are rewritten for local use.

## Cinematic Moment

- Example canvas: `1536x1024`
- Quality: `high`

```text
Create a cinematic frame of [scene].
Camera: [wide/medium/close-up], deliberate framing, clear subject placement.
Lighting: motivated light source, atmosphere, controlled contrast.
Story: one readable action or emotional beat.
Avoid: montage collage, random extra characters, fake subtitles.
```

## Animated Film Still

- Example canvas: `1536x1024`
- Quality: `high`

```text
Create an animated film still of [subject/action].
Style: polished stylized animation, appealing shapes, rich but controlled color.
Composition: strong foreground/midground/background separation.
Mood: [wonder/tension/humor/warmth].
Avoid: hyperreal rendering, unreadable background noise, text overlays.
```
