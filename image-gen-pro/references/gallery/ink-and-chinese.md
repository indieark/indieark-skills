# Ink & Chinese Gallery

> Load when: 需要水墨、国画、书卷气、留白、山水、花鸟或东方笔墨视觉。
> Avoid: 精确汉字排版、真实摄影、UI 或工业产品图。
> Pairs with: `../methods/style.md`、`../methods/composition.md`、`../methods/typography.md`。

Source: adapted from `gpt_image_2_skill` gallery category `Ink & Chinese`; prompts are rewritten for local use.

## Ink Landscape

- Size: `1536x1024`
- Quality: `high`

```text
Create an ink wash landscape of [mountain/river/scene].
Composition: layered distance, strong negative space, mist and paper texture.
Brushwork: varied ink density, dry brush accents, soft washes.
Mood: quiet, spacious, poetic.
Avoid: fake calligraphy, crowded buildings, glossy digital finish.
```

## Ink Object Study

- Size: `1024x1024`
- Quality: `medium`

```text
Create an ink study of [subject].
Focus: expressive brush silhouette, minimal strokes, paper grain.
Composition: subject balanced with empty space.
Palette: black ink with optional muted accent.
Avoid: realistic photo shading, decorative fake seals, generated text.
```
