# Fine Art Painting Gallery

> Load when: 需要古典绘画、油画、肖像、静物、风景或美术馆质感。
> Avoid: 指定在世艺术家精确风格、UI、技术图或真实产品照。
> Pairs with: `../methods/style.md`、`../methods/composition.md`、`../safety.md`。

Source: adapted from `gpt_image_2_skill` gallery category `Fine Art Painting`; prompts are rewritten for local use.

## Oil Portrait

- Size: `1024x1536`
- Quality: `high`

```text
Create an oil painting portrait of [subject].
Composition: classical bust or half-body, calm background, strong face focus.
Brushwork: visible layered paint, subtle skin tones, rich shadows.
Lighting: painterly directional light.
Avoid: naming living artists, fake signature, photographic smoothness.
```

## Still Life Painting

- Size: `1024x1024`
- Quality: `high`

```text
Create a fine art still life painting of [objects].
Arrangement: balanced tabletop composition, varied shapes and textures.
Lighting: warm directional light, deep but readable shadows.
Style: traditional paint texture, cohesive palette.
Avoid: modern logos, fake labels, cluttered object count.
```
