# Research Paper Figures Gallery

> Load when: 需要论文图、技术流程图、实验示意、机制图或 publication-style figure。
> Avoid: 营销图、自由插画、真实照片。
> Pairs with: `../methods/research-figures.md`、`../methods/structured-prompts.md`、`../methods/infographics.md`。

Source: adapted from `gpt_image_2_skill` gallery category `Research Paper Figures`; prompts are rewritten for local use.

## Pipeline Figure

- Example canvas: `1536x1024`
- Quality: `medium`

```text
Create a publication-style pipeline figure for [system/process].
Panels: A simple left-to-right workflow with 4-6 modules.
Visual grammar: clean boxes, arrows, minimal labels or blank label areas.
Style: white background, restrained colors, consistent line weight.
Avoid: invented numbers, dense paragraphs, decorative gradients.
```

## Experimental Setup

- Example canvas: `1536x1024`
- Quality: `medium`

```text
Create a research figure showing an experimental setup for [experiment].
Composition: apparatus or system layout with clear callout regions.
Labels: short generic labels or empty callout boxes.
Style: precise technical illustration, readable at slide size.
Avoid: fake data charts, impossible wiring, excessive decoration.
```
