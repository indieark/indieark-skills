## Genre Frame

- Size: `1536x1024`
- Quality: `high`

```text
Create a cinematic genre frame for [genre] about [scene].
Camera: [wide/close/low angle/top-down], strong blocking, clear subject.
Lighting: genre-appropriate contrast and color temperature.
Production design: environment details support the story.
Avoid: copying a specific film shot, fake subtitles, celebrity likeness.
```

- Quality: `medium`

```text
Create a storyboard-like cinematic frame of [action].
Composition: readable movement direction, clear foreground and background.
Mood: [tension/wonder/comedy/suspense].
```
