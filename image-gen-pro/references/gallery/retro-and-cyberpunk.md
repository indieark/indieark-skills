# Retro & Cyberpunk Gallery

> Load when: 需要复古未来、霓虹城市、80s/90s 科幻、赛博朋克界面或街景。
> Avoid: 严肃企业图、自然摄影或古典绘画。
> Pairs with: `../methods/style.md`、`../methods/composition.md`。

Source: adapted from `gpt_image_2_skill` gallery category `Retro & Cyberpunk`; prompts are rewritten for local use.

## Neon Street Scene

- Example canvas: `1536x1024`
- Quality: `high`

```text
Create a retro cyberpunk street scene of [subject].
Composition: wet reflective street, neon signs as abstract shapes, strong perspective lines.
Lighting: magenta/cyan neon, rim light, mist or rain haze.
Style: cinematic but readable, not over-cluttered.
Avoid: readable fake text, excessive signs, hidden main subject.
```

## Retro Tech Poster

- Example canvas: `1024x1536`
- Quality: `high`

```text
Create a retro-futurist poster for [concept].
Visual: bold machine or character silhouette, grid or chrome details.
Palette: limited neon accents over dark or muted base.
Layout: clean title-safe area if text will be added later.
Avoid: random typography, chaotic glow, too many competing focal points.
```
