# Architecture & Interior Gallery

> Load when: 需要建筑外观、室内设计、空间概念、房间 mood 或地产/场景视觉。
> Avoid: UI、角色、数据图或精确施工图。
> Pairs with: `../methods/composition.md`、`../methods/photography.md`、`../methods/style.md`。

Source: adapted from `gpt_image_2_skill` gallery category `Architecture & Interior`; prompts are rewritten for local use.

## Interior Concept

- Example canvas: `1536x1024`
- Quality: `high`

```text
Create an interior design concept for [room/use].
Layout: clear circulation, furniture placement, focal wall or feature.
Materials: [wood/stone/fabric/metal] with realistic texture.
Lighting: natural window light plus practical fixtures.
Avoid: impossible furniture scale, clutter, fake wall text.
```

## Architectural Exterior

- Example canvas: `1536x1024`
- Quality: `high`

```text
Create an architectural exterior image of [building type].
Form: clear massing, facade rhythm, entrance hierarchy.
Context: street/landscape/environment supports the building.
Lighting: [golden hour/overcast/night] with realistic shadows.
Avoid: distorted perspective, random signage, impossible structure.
```
