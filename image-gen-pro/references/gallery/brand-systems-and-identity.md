## Brand Mood Board

- Size: `1536x1024`
- Quality: `high`

```text
Create a brand identity mood board for [brand/product category].
Elements: color swatches, abstract marks, packaging sample, typography placeholders, image style tiles.
Style: coherent system, not a single logo.
Palette: [desired mood], accessible contrast.
Avoid: copying real brands, fake legal marks, unreadable text.
```
