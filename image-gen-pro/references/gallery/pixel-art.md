# Pixel Art Gallery

> Load when: 需要像素角色、道具、场景、tile、复古游戏素材或低分辨率风格。
> Avoid: 高真实摄影、复杂文字、论文图或精细产品照。
> Pairs with: `../methods/style.md`、`../methods/composition.md`、`../methods/product.md`。

Source: adapted from `gpt_image_2_skill` gallery category `Pixel Art`; prompts are rewritten for local use.

## Pixel Character / Item

- Example canvas: `1024x1024`
- Quality: `medium`

```text
Create pixel art of [character/item].
Silhouette: readable at small size, centered, limited animation-ready pose.
Palette: limited colors, clear highlights and shadows.
Background: flat transparent-ready or simple contrast.
Avoid: anti-aliased smooth painting, tiny unreadable details, text labels.
```

## Pixel Environment

- Example canvas: `1536x1024`
- Quality: `medium`

```text
Create a pixel art environment of [scene].
View: side-view, top-down or isometric as specified.
Tiles: clear ground, props, background layers.
Mood: [cozy/dangerous/magical/retro].
Avoid: mixed resolutions, blurry pixels, photorealistic lighting.
```
