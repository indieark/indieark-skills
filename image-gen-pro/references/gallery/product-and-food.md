# Product & Food Gallery

> Load when: 需要商品图、包装图、食物图、商业静物、卖点展示或材质细节参考。
> Avoid: 纯海报层级、UI mockup、论文图或局部编辑。
> Pairs with: `../methods/product.md`、`../methods/photography.md`、`../methods/style.md`。

Source: adapted from `gpt_image_2_skill` gallery category `Product & Food`; prompts are rewritten for local use.

## Patterns

### Premium Product Still Life

- Use when: 单个商品需要高级、干净、可上架的主图。
- Size: `1024x1024` or `1024x1536`
- Quality: `high`
- Prompt skeleton:

```text
Create a premium product still life for [product].
Composition: product centered or slightly off-center, clean negative space, readable silhouette.
Materials: describe surface, reflections, label placement, packaging edges.
Lighting: studio softbox, controlled highlights, natural shadow.
Background: minimal surface that supports the product color.
Avoid: extra logos, fake text, clutter, distorted packaging.
```

### Food Editorial Close-Up

- Use when: 食物需要真实诱人、餐厅菜单或社媒质感。
- Size: `1024x1536`
- Quality: `high`
- Prompt skeleton:

```text
Create an editorial food photograph of [dish].
Camera: close-up or three-quarter angle, shallow depth of field.
Food details: texture, steam, sauce, garnish, plate material.
Scene: simple tabletop with a few context props.
Lighting: warm natural window light, appetizing highlights.
Avoid: messy plate, unrealistic portions, unreadable text.
```

### Ingredient / Material Spread

- Use when: 展示食材、配方、香调、材质或制作过程。
- Size: `1536x1024`
- Quality: `medium` or `high`
- Prompt skeleton:

```text
Create a neatly arranged spread of [ingredients/materials].
Layout: top-down or organized grid, each element visually distinct.
Surface: [wood/stone/paper/metal] with realistic texture.
Color: balanced palette with one accent color.
Avoid: overlapping items, labels, messy shadows.
```

### Product In Use

- Use when: 商品需要放入使用场景，展示用途和生活方式。
- Size: `1536x1024`
- Quality: `high`
- Prompt skeleton:

```text
Create a lifestyle product image showing [product] being used in [context].
Subject: product remains the visual hero.
Scene: realistic environment, minimal props, believable human or usage cue if needed.
Lighting: natural and consistent with the environment.
Avoid: hiding the product, adding unrelated accessories, fake brand marks.
```
